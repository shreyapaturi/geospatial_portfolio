// Workflow

How I
work

01 Data audit & sourcing. Identify the right datasets — government open data, OSM, satellite — and assess quality, projection, and coverage gaps.
02 Python pipeline. GeoPandas for cleaning, reprojection, spatial joins and aggregation. GDAL for format conversion. All reproducible in a notebook.
03 QGIS analysis. Deep spatial analysis, topology validation, and iterative visual QA. Model Builder for repeatable workflows on large datasets.
04 Visualisation & output. Print-ready maps in QGIS, interactive Folium/Kepler dashboards for stakeholders, and charts via Matplotlib or Plotly.
05 Documentation. Every project ships with methodology notes, projection metadata, and data lineage — maps that can be peer-reviewed and reproduced.
